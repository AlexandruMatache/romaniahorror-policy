Privacy Policy

This application (RomaniaHorror-ROMV Automation Tool) does not collect, store, or share any personal information from external users.

The app is used solely by the TikTok account owner to generate and upload AI-created horror videos.

The application only interacts with TikTok's Content Posting API after the owner manually authorizes access.

No personal data from third-party users is processed, saved, transmitted, or analyzed. The tool operates strictly as a private automation system.

Only TikTok account credentials authorized by the owner are used, and they remain confidential.
